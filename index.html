Say what you want, but it was cool at the time, but unfortunately not the best for the environment, so I stopped...I became fairly adept at the hardware side of things and at this time I started making Roblox gaming content on YouTube. I have expanded my front-end knowledge and now have experience in back-end languages like Python, SQL and Java. I still use Codecademy to this day because it helps make everything click.
